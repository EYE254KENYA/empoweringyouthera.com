About Our Charity

Eye is dedicated to uplifting our community through various charitable initiatives that address the pressing needs of vulnerable populations. Our programs focus on providing essential resources, such as food and nutrition education, to ensure that families have access to healthy meals. We also strive to improve mental health among youths by offering peer counseling and support, helping them navigate challenges and build resilience. Additionally, Eye empowers young people through leadership programs that equip them with vital skills for personal and community growth. By fostering a sense of responsibility and encouraging active engagement, we aim to inspire the next generation of leaders who will contribute positively to our society. Together, we are committed to creating a lasting impact and building a healthier, more resilient community for all.
