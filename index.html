Our Team

Our team is the heart and soul of our mission. Each member brings passion, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to making a difference. Together, we work tirelessly to ensure that every effort, no matter how big or small, creates lasting change. From organizing outreach programs to building strong community connections, our team is driven by a shared vision of a brighter, more compassionate world. With their dedication, we turn challenges into opportunities, helping those in need with both kindness and action.

PROGRESS

COUNCELLING

At Eye, we prioritize mental well-being by implementing a peer counseling program, which empowers youths to support each other, fostering a sense of community and resilience while providing crucial guidance and resources.

FOOD SUPPLY

Eye is committed to combating hunger by supplying donated food to homes and schools, ensuring that vulnerable communities have access to nutritious meals and support.

NUTRITION EDUCATION

At Eye, we prioritize community health by providing nutrition education, empowering individuals with knowledge about balanced diets and healthy meal preparation to promote overall well-being.

YOUTH LEADERSHIP PROGRAMS

Eye’s Youth Leadership Program empowers young people by developing their leadership skills and engaging them in community service, fostering a sense of responsibility and connection to local issues.
